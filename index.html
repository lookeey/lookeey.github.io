What? How? Where?

What?

BOMB on Fantom is a deflationary token. 1% of each BOMB transaction is burned. It is one of the first independently launched Opera tokens, and independent developers are presently working on several different use cases utilizing its deflationary nature. BOMB is a community-driven token which seeks to cement its place on the Fantom Opera blockchain.

How?

BOMB is made possible by the speed, security, and scalability of the Fantom Opera blockchain. Trading BOMB is near-free and instant. The Opera blockchain is perfect for the development of decentralized applications.

Where?

You can get BOMB tokens by swapping your Fantom tokens on SushiSwap.

How can I do that?

You will need to create a Fantom Wallet, connect it to Metamask, and swap your FTM for BOMB on Sushiswap.
Make sure to set at least 2% slippage, for the 1% burning.
