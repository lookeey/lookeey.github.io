What? How? Where?

What?

BOMB is a deflationary token on the blockchain that burns 1% of every transaction upon confirmation.
It is one of the first projects being launched on the Fantom Blockchain

How?

The BOMB token is made possible by the technology of Fantom, a secure EVM compatible chain that allows for the lowest fees and greatest scalability; perfect for the development of decentralized applications.

Where?

You can get BOMB tokens by swapping your Fantom tokens on SushiSwap.

How can I do that?

You will need to create a Fantom Wallet, connect it to Metamask, and swap your FTM for BOMB on Sushiswap.
Make sure to set at least 2% slippage, for the 1% burning.
